My name is Justice Murphy, but you can call me Pandora. I'm 16 years old, and as you may have guessed, I love reading! I started this blog during the summer of my junior year to share my love of books with the world (and also motivate myself to read more.) I'm black, queer, and young, and I always get excited when I see others like me engaging in literature and other media adjacent to it, so I also started this blog to put my voice out there!
